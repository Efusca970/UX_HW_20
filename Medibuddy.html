Medibuddy
Case Study

Your Medication Reminder Friend

ABOUT THE PROJECT

Problem: Many users struggle to manage their medicine and vitamins on a daily basis often leading to incorrect or even missed doses. Solution: We believe by designing an application that assists the users with their daily medication and vitamin routine, we can also offer a healthier way of living. Make Medibuddy your gentle reminder buddy.

DISCIPLINES & TOOLS

User Research (Interviews + Surveys) & Analysis, Persona Creation, User Experience Design (UI/UX), Interaction Design, Prototyping, In-person User Testing, Figma, InVision, Miro, Trello

MY TEAM

MY ROLL

My role for this project was that of a UI/UX Designer

My responsibilities were - > Defining the scope & Strategy + Planning the project > Conducting User Research (User Interviews and Surveys) > Delivering solutions through UX Design methods and techniques > Collaborate on the Mobile Prototypes

RESEARCH

INTERVIEW PLAN

We started our interview plan with 3 interviews to help discover what concerns users faced when taking their daily medications, vitamins and supplements. We discovered that many users tended to forget and even missed dosage. We surveyed 91 participants, aging from 20-60 years old, to gain a broader perspective on their daily medication, and vitamin routines. • Remind them of doses • Refills of prescriptions • Discounts of medications • Correct and clear instructions Doing this will give our users options to help keep their medication routine on track.

QUANTITATIVE SURVEY DATA

Wider user base with the inclusion of vitamins and supplements.

At least 81% of testers took at least 1 vitamin a day.

We found that 72% of testers look for ways to save on their prescriptions, vitamins and supplements.

IDEATION

From our interviews, we listened to the needs of people who take muliple medications, supllements or vitamins. We learned from key takeways and organized them into a affinity map.

Key Takeaways:

  • Most people need visual reminders
  • Users usually take a vitamin or supplements
  • Prices of Vitamins, Medications or Supplements are a factor
  • Subscription help
  • User like Health information

I Like, I Wish, Whaf if - Affinity Map

From here we used our chart to create prioritization matrix, to understand what the use will need, the most using this app.

Key Takeaways for highest priority and low complexity:

  • Ease of use
  • Reminders
  • Refill reminders
  • Amazon connect accounts

From our research we created this user persona, based on people we interviewed. We drew insight from talking to them personally, and getting an image of the ideal user of this app. Here is Dan Finkelburg.

His Frustrations are:

  • Forgets to Take Medication and Vitamins
  • Find Medications Expensive

His Goals and Motivations are:

  • Better Health
  • lose Weight
  • Healthier Lifestyle
